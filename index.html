Ice Lemon Tea

In this sultry summer, would you like to have some cool drinks to quench your thirst? Let me introduce a summer cooler --- Ice lemon tea for you. It is cold and refreshing. Lemons rich in vitamin C . It maintains our skin healthy and help us to loss our weight . Furthermore, antioxidants in both tea and lemon can prevent the mutation of cells which will lead to the cancer. There are a lot of benefits on drinking ice lemon tea. Click here to look for the further advantages of lemon tea. Actually, we can make it easily in our home. The ingredient is easy to be found and don't need a longer time to prepare for it.




Ingredient

4 black tea bags, lemon juice, ice cubes, lemon slides, 1000ml water, sugar



Procedure

Step 1

Wash the lemon and cut it into the slides.

Step 2

Boil 1000ml water and add some sugar into the boiling water.

Step 3

Stir the sugar until it dissolved in the water properly.

Step 4

Steep the tea bag into the hot water and wait for 15 minutes.

Step 5

Add some lemon juice in the tea and mix it.

Step 6

Fill the glass with the ice cubes.

Step 6

Put some lemon slides on the side of the glass for decoration and get ready to serve it.
